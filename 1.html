🧑‍🏫Unit 1 Exercise 解説メール

こんにちは！Unit 1 のエクササイズの解説だよ。一緒に見ていこう！✨

1.1 What's happening in the pictures? 🤔

この問題では、絵を見て、現在進行形 (Present Continuous: am/is/are + -ing形) を使って、それぞれの人物が「今」何をしているかを説明するよ。与えられた動詞の中から正しいものを選んで、適切な形にしよう！

ポイント！ 現在進行形は 「今まさに～している」という状況を表すときに使うよ。主語に合わせて be動詞 (am/is/are) を選ぶのを忘れずにね！

  • ✅ 1. She's taking a picture. (写真を撮っている)
  • ✅ 2. He's tying a shoelace. (靴ひもを結んでいる)
  • ✅ 3. They're crossing the road. (道を渡っている)
  • ✅ 4. He's scratching his head. (頭をかいている)
  • ✅ 5. She's hiding behind a tree. (木の後ろに隠れている)
  • ✅ 6. They're waving to somebody. (誰かに手を振っている)

1.2 Sentence Matching 🧩

ここでは、左側の文 (1-8) と、それに続くのに最も自然な右側の文 (a-h) を組み合わせるよ。文脈を考えて、意味がつながるペアを見つけよう！

💡 ヒント: 各ペアの文は、理由や状況説明になっていることが多いよ。

左の文 (状況) 組み合わせる記号 右の文 (理由・説明)
1. Please don't make so much noise. f I'm trying to work.
2. We need to leave soon. e It's getting late.
3. I don't have anywhere to live right now. g I'm staying with friends.
4. I need to eat something soon. a I'm getting hungry.
5. They don't need their car any more. d They're trying to sell it.
6. Things are not so good at work. h The company is losing money.
7. It isn't true what they say. b They're lying.
8. We're going to get wet. c It's starting to rain.

1.3 Write questions (Present Continuous) ❓

ここでは、与えられた状況とヒントの単語を使って、現在進行形の疑問文を作る練習だよ。「(疑問詞 +) am/is/are + 主語 + -ing形?」の形を思い出そう！

  • 👉 1. What's all that noise? (what / happen?)
    ⇒ What's happening?
  • 👉 2. What's the matter? (why / you / cry?)
    ⇒ Why are you crying?
  • 👉 3. Where's your mother? (she / work / today?)
    ⇒ Is she working today?
  • 👉 4. I haven't seen you for ages. (what / you / do / these days?)
    ⇒ What are you doing these days?
  • 👉 5. Amy is a student. (what / she / study?)
    ⇒ What's she studying? / What is she studying?
  • 👉 6. Who are those people? (what / they / do?)
    ⇒ What are they doing?
  • 👉 7. I heard you started a new job. (you / enjoy / it?)
    ⇒ Are you enjoying it?
  • 👉 8. We're not in a hurry. (why / you / walk / so fast?)
    ⇒ Why are you walking so fast?

1.4 Put the verb into the correct form (Present Continuous +/-) 👍👎

最後のセクション！文脈に合わせて、動詞を現在進行形の肯定文 (am/is/are + -ing) または否定文 (am not/isn't/aren't + -ing) にするよ。

ポイント！ 文全体の意味をよく読んで、肯定 (している) か否定 (していない) かを判断しよう。「now」「these days」「today」などの時間を示す言葉や、文脈 (例: ラジオを消していい → 聞いていない) がヒントになるよ！

  • ● 1. Please don't make so much noise. I'm trying (I / try) to work. (解答例)
  • ● 2. Let's go out now. It isn't raining (it / rain / negative) any more. (解答例)
  • ● 3. You can turn off the radio. I'm not listening (I / listen / negative) to it.
  • ● 4. Kate phoned last night. She's on holiday with friends. She's having (She / have) a great time and doesn't want to come back.
  • ● 5. Andrew started evening classes recently. He's learning (He / learn) Japanese.
  • ● 6. Paul and Sarah have had an argument and now they aren't speaking (they / speak / negative) to one another.
  • ● 7. The situation is already very bad and now it's getting (it / get) worse.
  • ● 8. Tim isn't working (work / negative) today. He's taken the day off.
  • ● 9. I'm looking (I / look) for Sophie. Do you know where she is?
  • ● 10. The washing machine has been repaired. It's working (It / work) now.
  • ● 11. They're building (They / build) a new hospital. It will be finished next year.
  • ● 12. Ben is a student, but he's not very happy. He isn't enjoying (He / enjoy / negative) his course.
  • ● 13. I think it's going to rain. The weather is changing (The weather / change). Look at those clouds.
  • ● 14. Dan has been in the same job for a long time. He's starting (He / start) to get bored with it.
